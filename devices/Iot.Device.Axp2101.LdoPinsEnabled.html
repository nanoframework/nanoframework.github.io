LDO channel enable flags from LDO_ONOFF_CTRL0 (0x90) and LDO_ONOFF_CTRL1 (0x91) registers. Bits [7:0] of register 0x90 map to ALDO1-4, BLDO1-2, CPUSLDO, DLDO1. Bit 0 of register 0x91 maps to DLDO2.
