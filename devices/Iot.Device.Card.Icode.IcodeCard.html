Class IcodeCard

Namespace
Iot.Device.Card.Icode

An ICODE card class. Supports ICODE SLIX, ICODE SLIX2, ICODE DNA, ICODE 3. Implements ISO/IEC 15693 commands for reading, writing, and managing vicinity cards.
