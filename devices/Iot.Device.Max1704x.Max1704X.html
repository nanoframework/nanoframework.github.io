QuickStart()

A quick-start allows the MAX17043 to restart fuel-gauge calculations in the same manner as initial power-up of the IC. If an application’s power-up sequence is exceedingly noisy such that excess error is introduced into the IC’s “first guess” of SOC, the host can issue a quick-start to reduce the error. A quick-start is initiated by a rising edge on the QSTRT pin, or through software by writing 4000h to MODE register. Note: on the MAX17048/49 this will also clear / disable EnSleep.

Sleep()

Puts the device into sleep mode. In sleep mode, the IC halts all operations, reducing current consumption to below 1μA. After exiting sleep mode, the IC continues normal operation. In sleep mode, the IC does not detect self-discharge. If the battery changes state while the IC sleeps, the IC cannot detect it, causing SOC error. Wake up the IC before charging or discharging.
